We are a team of five students majoring in Mechanics from Houston International College of Dalian Maritime University. We named our team "Victory." "Victory" is not just a name, it represents our firm belief and determination to pursue victory in our graduation design. As a close-knit team, each of us has accumulated a wealth of knowledge and skills in the mechanical field, and we have brought these resources together to successfully complete our graduation project. We understand that to be successful in the field of mechanical engineering, we need to keep learning, innovating, and challenging ourselves. We believe that through cooperation and perseverance, we can overcome all difficulties and achieve excellence.

Project Introduction

This graduation project focuses on the field of mechanical engineering and its role in addressing environmental challenges and improving ecological conditions. The project aims to explore sustainable solutions that can contribute to a better world by mitigating environmental degradation and promoting a harmonious relationship with nature.
